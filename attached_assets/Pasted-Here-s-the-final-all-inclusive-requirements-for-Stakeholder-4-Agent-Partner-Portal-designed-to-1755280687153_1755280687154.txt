Here’s the final, all-inclusive requirements for Stakeholder 4: Agent / Partner Portal — designed to empower your “Compliance Rakshaks” or any third-party partners to generate leads, track commissions, and collaborate without exposing sensitive backend data.


---

DigiComply – Final Requirements for Agent / Partner Portal

Vision: A secure, role-restricted platform that enables agents to generate leads, track their performance, access marketing resources, and stay updated on client progress without compromising operational or confidential data.


---

1. Access & Authentication

1. Secure Agent Login

Username/password with OTP verification.

Role-based restrictions (Agent, Regional Manager, etc.).



2. Agent Profile

Name, contact details, assigned territory, joining date.



3. Territory & Service Access Control

Agents can only view leads/clients in their assigned geography or segment.





---

2. Lead & Client Management

4. Lead Submission Form

Fields: Client name, contact info, entity type, required services.

File upload option for basic KYC docs.



5. Lead Status Tracking

Status stages: New → Contacted → Converted → In Progress → Closed.



6. Client Progress View

High-level status of services (e.g., “KYC complete”, “Incorporation filed”).

No access to sensitive documents or internal ops notes.



7. Lead Assignment

Ability to transfer leads to other agents (with admin approval).





---

3. Commission & Earnings Tracking

8. Commission Dashboard

Total commission earned, pending payouts, and cleared amounts.



9. Earning Breakdown

Commission per client/service with date.



10. Payout Schedule

Display of upcoming payment dates.



11. Commission Dispute Submission

Form to raise issues regarding payout discrepancies.





---

4. Sales Enablement

12. Marketing Collateral Library

Downloadable brochures, presentations, WhatsApp creatives.



13. Product Training Modules

Service explainers, compliance basics, selling scripts.



14. Pitch Decks

Ready-to-use decks for client presentations.



15. Success Stories & Case Studies

Shareable examples of successful client cases.





---

5. Communication & Support

16. Agent–Admin Chat

Direct messaging with the admin team for clarifications.



17. Support Ticketing

Raise requests/issues and track their resolution.



18. Announcement Board

Admin can post important updates, promotions, or law changes.





---

6. Performance Analytics

19. Lead Conversion Metrics

Number of leads submitted, converted, and lost.



20. Top Services Sold

Breakdown of which services the agent sells most.



21. Monthly Performance Trends

Graphs of leads, conversions, and commission earned.



22. Leaderboard (Optional)

Gamified ranking to encourage competition among agents.





---

7. Automation & Follow-Ups

23. Lead Nurturing Automation

System auto-sends WhatsApp/email follow-ups to unconverted leads after X days.



24. Reminder System

Alerts for pending lead actions (e.g., follow-up calls).



25. Inactive Lead Revival

Auto-flag dormant leads for re-engagement.





---

8. Integration & Data Handling

26. CRM Sync

Integration with core DigiComply CRM for real-time lead updates.



27. Data Privacy Controls

Agents see only the minimal client data required for sales.



28. Audit Log

Every agent action logged with timestamp for compliance.





---

9. Future-Ready Enhancements

29. Referral Sub-Agent Model

Agents can onboard sub-agents and earn overrides.



30. Incentive Program Tracking

Track qualification for bonuses and rewards.



31. Geo-Tagging of Leads

Auto-record lead location for territory compliance.



32. AI Lead Scoring

System ranks leads by likelihood of conversion.





---

10. Security & Compliance

33. IP & Device Restrictions

Limit portal access to approved devices or IPs.



34. Two-Factor Authentication

Required for commission withdrawals.



35. Encrypted Data Handling

All client and lead data encrypted in transit and at rest.
